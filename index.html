MY ENGINES

Forge Engine

An engine made for a Year 2 & 3 game project.

The editor's interface and various functionalities are focused on future-proofing and extendibility.

Technology implemented here by me include C# scripting integration, prefab technology, memory management, and adaptive audio.

Forge Engine was used to develop Howl: A Tale of Wolves.

Beta Engine

An engine made for a Year 1 game project.

The editor's interface and various functionalities are focused on building the game rapidly.

Technology implemented here by me include inheritance-based Entity-Component System, editor interfaces, and a job delay system.

Beta Engine was used to develop Spellthief.

MY GAMES

Howl: A Tale of Wolves

Howl: A Tale of Wolves is a single-player platformer set in the harsh yet beautiful wilderness. In this interactive experience, players take control of an adult wolf and her young pup who have just had their home destroyed. Together, they brave rough terrains and overcome dangerous obstacles through the seasons - but will it be enough to finally reach paradise across the mountains?

3rd —  Claude Comair Game of the Year, DigiPen (Singapore) Awards
Best Sophomore Game, DigiPen (Singapore) Awards
Best Sophomore Technology, DigiPen (Singapore) Awards

Finalist — Best 2D Graphics Technology, DigiPen (Singapore) Awards
Finalist — Most Innovative Design, DigiPen (Singapore) Awards

Gameplay implemented here by me include UI programming, animation transitions, and bug fixing.

Howl: A Tale of Wolves was developed using Forge Engine.

Spellthief

Spellthief is a fast paced action platformer where you simultaneously play as both Clyde the thief, as well as Oliver the mage. As you fight your way through the relentless waves of mobs and gain new skills, uncover the dark secret that ties the two unbeknownst adventurers.

Best Freshman Technology from ST Engineering, DigiPen (Singapore) Awards

Finalist — Best 2D Physics Technology from Continental Singapore, DigiPen (Singapore) Awards
Finalist — Best Artificial Intelligence from Ubisoft Singapore, DigiPen (Singapore) Awards
Finalist — Best Music and Sound Design, DigiPen (Singapore) Awards

Gameplay implemented here by me include UI programming, animation transitions, AI, and bug fixing.

Spellthief was developed using Beta Engine.
